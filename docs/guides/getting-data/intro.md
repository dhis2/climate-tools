title: Download data
---

An important challenge when working with climate data is actually obtaining the data. Climate data is hosted across various sources and hosts, and can be difficult and time-consuming to access. 

In this section we provide practical guides and notebooks demonstrating how the `dhis2eo` library, together with other libraries included in DHIS2 Climate Tools, can be used to access commonly used climate datasets.
